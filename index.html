Text Mining

There is a lot of information in the text that we create and keep in our businesses, but extracting useful information from it is difficult. We have expertise in extracting data from text; whether it comes from an online social network, survey responses, a collection of books in the office, or transcripts of meetings with clients. Our techniques can perform exploratory analysis, such as looking for general themes or trends, or to answer specific questions you may have about the data.




Real Time Reporting

Get real-time, on-demand reporting, either through our web portal, or self-hosted on your systems. You can also get alerted when things don't look right, or when things are going really well! Combined with regular summary reporting, you can get a full picture of your business at any point of time.


Data Integration

Often, the data you need for your organisation comes from external sources. We have extensive experience in finding, shaping, analysing and automating the collection of data from a variety of resources, both online and offline. We can also help with collecting data from customer surveys, website logs and other sources.
